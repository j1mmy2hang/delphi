# Acknowledgments

Delphi's prismatic "thinking wave" is derived from the **PrismaticBurst** background
from **[react-bits](https://reactbits.dev)** — ported to a Metal shader on iOS
(`ios/Delphi/Shaders/PrismaticBurst.metal`) and rendered via `ogl` on the web
(`web/src/components/PrismaticBurst.tsx`).

react-bits is used to build this app, which its license permits. The components
themselves are not sold, sublicensed, or redistributed.
