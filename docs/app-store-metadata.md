Ask a question and a single, considered reply forms out of a living prismatic wave — no chat bubbles, no feed, no clutter. It's built for reflection, not doom-scrolling: one thought at a time, beautifully.

WHY DELPHI
• One message at a time — a calm, focused surface instead of an endless thread.

Type what's on your mind. Delphi thinks, then a reply materializes at the center of the stage. Start again anytime.
